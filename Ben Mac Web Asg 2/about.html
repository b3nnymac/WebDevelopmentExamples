Who am I?

My name is Ben Maciorowski. I currently pursuing Mount Royal University's BCIS. I've already obtained a Bachelors and Masters of arts in Film and Media Studies from the University of Alberta in Edmonton and York University in Toronto, respectively. Check out a fun video project I worked on for a lark. The reason I've started this new program is because I enjoy technology and it's ability to enrich lives. I don't know specifically what I would like to with the BCIS, but I would like to be involved with an organization that is concerned with environmental sustainability, food security, and surround myself with progressive creative thinkers. I have no desire to hold any position in the oil and gas industry. The reason I returned to Calgary was originally because my partner accepted a teaching contract at a Sikh school in North East Calgary. I was very reluctant to return to Calgary, but I am pleasantly surprised with the forwarder thinking I have encountered, although there is room for more.
